The Number Grid Challenge (8-Puzzle)

Goal: Arrange the tiles in order 1 through 8.

How to Play

  1. The empty cell (blank space) is always the final position of the number 9.
  2. Click on any tile that is **horizontally or vertically adjacent** to the blank space.
  3. The tile will slide into the blank space, increasing your move count.
  4. The puzzle is complete when all numbers are in sequential order (1 to 8).
